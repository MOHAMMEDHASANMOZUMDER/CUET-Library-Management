About the System

The CUET Library Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to modernize and streamline library operations at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology. Developed by a team of dedicated computer science students, this system addresses the evolving needs of modern academic libraries.

Our system eliminates traditional barriers to library access by providing online services for book pre-booking, fine payments, study note sharing, and real-time availability checking. This digital transformation reduces physical crowding, saves time, and enhances the overall library experience for students and staff.

Key Features

Smart Book Management

Real-time tracking of book availability, automated cataloging, and intelligent search capabilities.

Mobile-First Design

Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly on all devices, from smartphones to desktops.

Digital Payments

Integrated mobile banking support for fine payments through bKash, Nagad, and Rocket.

Collaborative Learning
